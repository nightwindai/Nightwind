FAQ

Common questions.

Does nightwind.ai require cloud connectivity?

No. nightwind.ai is fully airgapped. All processing occurs on customer-owned infrastructure inside the customer's own security perimeter. No data ever leaves the facility.

What languages does nightwind.ai support?

Any language. There is no language limitation. Operators can query in English, Arabic, French, Mandarin, Russian, Spanish, or any other language. Mixed-language archives are supported.

What is the licensing model?

Perpetual licence. No subscription fees, no per-seat charges, no recurring vendor dependency. After deployment, customers own all components.

Is nightwind.ai suitable for classified networks?

Yes. nightwind.ai is designed for classified and sensitive environments. Zero network dependencies, no telemetry, no vendor call-home. Compatible with air-gapped and classified network environments.

What document formats does nightwind.ai support?

PDFs, Word documents (.docx), and plain text files. No reformatting or conversion required — archives are ingested as-is. Querying is available once the initial ingestion and indexing process is complete.

Does nightwind.ai require ongoing subscriptions?

No. Perpetual licence. No recurring fees, no per-seat charges, no renewal negotiations. Customers own all deployed components outright.
